Honours Degree
B.Sc. (Hons.) in Fire & Industrial Safety
4 Years / 8 Semesters
Program Overview
A comprehensive 4-year honours degree with a research component in the final year. Covers fire science, safety engineering, occupational health, disaster management, environmental safety, and industrial risk assessment — aligned to industry-certified safety training and ISO 45001 frameworks.
Eligibility
  • 10+2 / Intermediate / Diploma (2 or 3 Years) — any stream or equivalent

B.Sc. in Fire & Industrial Safety
3 Years / 6 Semesters
