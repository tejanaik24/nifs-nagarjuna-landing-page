Welcome to!

Fire and Industrial Safety
Department, ANU

The Department of Fire and Industrial Safety was established on 18th September, 2025 by the esteemed personalities Sri. Dr. Gangadhara Rao Kancharla (I/c Vice Chancellor), Smt. Prof. K. Ratna Shiela Mani garu (Rector I/C), Sri Prof. G. Simhachalam garu (Registrar I/C), and Sri Suneel Mahanty (C.E.O. and Chairman), offering Bachelor Degrees in Fire and Industrial Safety on self-supporting mode.

Technically collaborated with the National Institute of Fire and Safety (NIFS), the department provides sophisticated classrooms, digital labs, and advanced fire safety training facilities — equipping students to achieve global standards of excellence.

Sri. SUNEEL MAHANTY

CEO & Chairman, NIFS
"

Welcome to the Department of Fire and Industrial Safety at Acharya Nagarjuna University — a department born from the conviction that safety is not just a profession, but a responsibility that saves lives and builds nations.

Our programs blend rigorous academic training with real-world skill development under NSDC, preparing graduates who are not merely employees — but leaders who champion safety culture in industries across India and beyond. With 85+ NIFS centres operating nationally, our students step into a ready network of opportunity, mentorship and career growth.
